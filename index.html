My favorite baseball team is the Los Angles Dodgers.The Dodgers first started in Brooklyn.They were established in 1883 in Brooklyn, New York, the team moved to Los Angeles before the 1958 season. They played for four seasons at the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum before moving to their current home of Dodger Stadium in 1962. The Dodgers as a franchise have won six World Series titles and 21 National League pennants.
